What We Do

We are a pet adoption service specializing in finding loving homes for chunky, affectionate cats. It's our job to:

  • Curate Perfectly Plump Companions

    We specialize in pairing loving families with affectionate, chunky feline friends who bring joy and warmth to any home.

  • Promote Fat Cat Health and Happiness

    Our cats are delightfully fluffy, and we ensure their well-being through balanced diets, proper care, and regular veterinary support.

  • Provide Guidance

    From choosing your new feline friend to adjusting to life together, we offer expert advice and resources every step of the way.
